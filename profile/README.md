## About Trinix

Trinix is a next-generation **AI-driven Discord ecosystem** built for **performance, safety, and engagement**.  
It combines autonomous moderation tools, deep economy simulations, and intelligent automation to help servers grow faster while staying secure.

### Core Systems

- **AI Moderation Engine**  
  Detects harmful content, scams, and spam with adaptive scoring models and multi-layer safety rules.

- **Live Report System**  
  Automatically reviews reports, logs actions, and verifies context with transparent review tracking.

- **Economy & Stock Market Simulator**  
  A full-featured in-server economy with jobs, trading, car systems, IPOs, and auctions.

- **Server Automation**  
  Smart utilities that handle welcome flows, content cleanup, auto-role assignment, and scheduled announcements.
